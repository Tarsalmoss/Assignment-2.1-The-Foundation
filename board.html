Boards

1. Design and complete robotic and embedded systems solutions that address real-world situations and challenges.

2. Communicate effectively with diverse audiences to gather requirements and explain technical solutions.

3. Use industry standard tools and technologies to develop and implement robotic and embedded systems.

4. Develop and implement machine learning and artificial intelligence solutions for robotic systems.

5. Understand and apply electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and computer science concepts in the creation of robotic systems.

6. Demonstrate professionalism, including skills such as teamwork, leadership, and project management.

The Robotics degree program at UAT prepares students to design, build, program, and integrate robotic and autonomous systems.

  • Design and construct robotic systems that integrate mechanical components, electronics, and software.
  • Apply embedded programming to control microprocessors and robotic systems.
  • Demonstrate skills in automation, control systems, and sensors for autonomous robots.
  • Analyze and troubleshoot robotic hardware and software problems.
  • Implement machine learning or AI techniques to improve robotic decision-making.
  • Collaborate in multidisciplinary teams to plan, design, and execute robotics projects.
